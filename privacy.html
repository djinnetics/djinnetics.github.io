Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tomasz Król App Development ("we," "us," or "our") collects, stores, uses, and protects your personal data in connection with your use of the Djinnetics mobile application (the "App") and our website (collectively, the "Services").

By using the Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Personal Data We Collect

We collect data to provide you with a personalized AI fitness experience. This includes:

1.1 Data You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, age, and subscription details.
  • Physical Profile: Gender, height, weight, fitness level, workout preferences, equipment availability, and physical limitations or injuries you choose to disclose.
  • Communications: Content of your messages when you contact support or interact with our social media channels.
  • AI Coach Conversations: When you chat with the AI Coach (powered by Google Gemini), we process and store the text of your conversations to provide relevant fitness guidance.

1.2 Data Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Details about how you use the App, such as features accessed, time spent, workout logs, and progress statistics.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID), time zone, and IP address.
  • Log Data: Server logs including crash reports, system activity, and timestamp of access.
  • Transaction Data: Purchase history and subscription status (processed securely by Google Play or RevenueCat; we do not store your full credit card details).

Note: Unlike some other fitness apps, we do not collect body scan photos, use camera movement tracking, or integrate with Apple HealthKit/Google Health Connect at this time.

2. Purposes and Legal Bases for Processing

We process your personal data for specific purposes based on legal grounds (GDPR/UK GDPR):

Purpose Legal Basis
To provide and maintain the Services (e.g., generating workout plans, AI chat). Performance of Contract
To improve and develop new features (e.g., analyzing usage trends via Firebase). Legitimate Interest
To process payments and subscriptions. Performance of Contract
To provide customer support. Legitimate Interest
To ensure security and prevent fraud. Legitimate Interest / Legal Obligation
To serve relevant advertisements (via Google Ads). Consent / Legitimate Interest
To comply with legal obligations (e.g., tax laws). Legal Obligation

3. Sharing Your Personal Data (Processors)

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with trusted third-party service providers ("Processors") who assist us in operating the App:

  • Infrastructure & Database: Google Firebase (Firestore) – USA/Global. Stores user data and chat history.
  • AI Intelligence: Google Cloud Vertex AI (Gemini models) – Global. Processes workout logic and chat interactions.
  • Analytics & Advertising: Google Firebase Analytics & Google Ads – USA. Helps us understand app usage and measure ad performance.
  • Payments: Google Play (Google) & RevenueCat – USA. Handles subscriptions and in-app purchase validation.

These providers process data strictly in accordance with our instructions and applicable privacy laws.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We and our partners use cookies, Software Development Kits (SDKs), and similar technologies (e.g., Firebase SDK) to:

  • Authenticate your identity.
  • Analyze app performance and crashes.
  • Deliver relevant advertisements (unless you opt-out).

Advertising ID: You can reset your device’s Advertising ID or opt-out of personalized ads in your device settings (Settings > Privacy > Tracking/Ads).

5. International Data Transfers

Our Services are hosted on Google Cloud Platform (Firebase), primarily in the United States and other global locations. If you access the App from the European Economic Area (EEA), UK, or Switzerland, your data may be transferred outside your jurisdiction. We rely on Google's Data Processing Addendum and Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) to ensure your data remains protected in compliance with GDPR.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary:

  • Active Accounts: We keep your data while your account is active to provide the Services.
  • Deleted Accounts: If you request account deletion, we generally delete your personal data within 30 days, ensuring all backups are purged.
  • Legal Obligations: We may retain transaction records (e.g., purchase history) for longer periods (up to 7 years) to comply with tax and accounting laws.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location (EU, UK, California, etc.), you have specific rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • Restriction/Objection: Restrict or object to certain processing (e.g., for marketing).
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time where we rely on it.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at whataking.development@gmail.com. We may verify your identity before fulfilling requests.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Services are strictly intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the account immediately.

9. Security

We implement robust technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest (encrypted database storage). However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email. The "Effective date" at the top of this page indicates when the latest changes took effect.
